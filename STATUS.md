## Risks and Mitigations
*   **Risk:** Complexity of 5+ set diagrams may result in cluttered visuals.
    *   **Mitigation:** Investigate alternative layout algorithms (e.g., ellipses or non-circular shapes) for higher-order diagrams.
*   **Risk:** Browser compatibility issues with advanced SVG features.
    *   **Mitigation:** Limit SVG usage to standard primitives and test across major browsers.
