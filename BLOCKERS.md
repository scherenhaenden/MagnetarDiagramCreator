## Escalation Process
If a blocker remains in `Active` status for more than **24 hours** without a clear path to resolution:
1.  Raise the issue in the daily standup or team chat.
2.  Assign a senior engineer or architect to pair-program on the resolution.
3.  Update the `STATUS.md` Risks section.
